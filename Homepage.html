Dewey's Biography

29 days after Dewey was able to to legally control alcohol, alcohol controlled Dewey to make a very bad decision. This isn't an addiction story, rather a tale of a very successful hero, forced to reasses his goals. Driving him, was this love for adventure. The reassesment took pace after a car hit him while he had a BAC of 0.27. His unconcious body lay defensless on the snowy street as a separate car ran over his body. Luckily, the hero of this story was capable of reassesing his goals. In hidsight, it's easy to see the multitude of opportunities he had to do just that.

In the following links you will find how this boy dealt with overcoming his DAI (diffused Axonoal Injury. This injury is a shearing on the train, that is coupled with TBI (traumatic Brain Injury). Docotrs said that it would be a miricle if he was able to feed himself again. However, Dewey had other plans for himself.

The accident happened February 13, and in May, Dewey took a college course that he recieved a B in. That Fall, Dewey took another class and recieved a B+ in it. Finally, he took a condensed winter course that had a test every single day, even weekends. He got an A- in that course. With the encouragment of his biggest cheerleader, his Mother, Dewey continued on to take three courses that Spring that he is doing very well in.

Now, Dewey will tell you about his recovery process. When one's actions are funneled towards a goal, actions speak louder than words. Soon, you will see the unstoppable force one aquires when this happens.
